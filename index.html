CAFÉ CIEL IS DELIGHTED TO ANNOUNCE AN EXCLUSIVE COLLABORATION WITH THE ICONIC MAGNOLIA BAKERY, BRINGING TOGETHER REFINED CAFÉ CULTURE AND WORLD RENOWNED CLASSIC DESSERTS. THIS LIMITED EDITION PARTNERSHIP BLENDS CAFÉ CIEL'S ELEGANT ATMOSPHERE AND ARTISANAL BEVERAGES WITH MAGNOLIA'S SIGNATURE SWEET CREATIONS, OFFERING GUESTS A TRULY INDULGENT EXPERIENCE.

AT THE HEART OF THE COLLABORATION IS A CURATED MENU THAT REIMAGINES MAGNOLIA BAKERY'S MOST BELOVED TREATS THROUGH CAFÉ CIEL'S CONTEMPORARY LENS. GUESTS CAN ENJOY PERFECTLY PAIRED COFFEE AND DESSERT COMBINATIONS, FROM VELVETY LATTES ALONGSIDE THE FAMOUS BANANA PUDDING TO DELICATE PASTRIES INSPIRED BY MAGNOLIA'S CLASSIC CUPCAKES.

THE SPACE ITSELF WILL REFLECT THE UNION OF BOTH BRANDS COMBINING CAFÉ CIEL'S SERENE, MODERN AESTHETIC WITH MAGNOLIA BAKERY'S NOSTALGIC CHARM. EXPECT SOFT PASTEL TONES, INVITING TEXTURES, AND A WARM, WELCOMING AMBIANCE DESIGNED FOR BOTH CASUAL VISITS AND SPECIAL MOMENTS.

THIS COLLABORATION CELEBRATES CRAFTSMANSHIP, QUALITY INGREDIENTS, AND THE JOY OF SIMPLE INDULGENCE. WHETHER YOU'RE STOPPING BY FOR A MORNING COFFEE OR AN AFTERNOON TREAT, CAFÉ CIEL X MAGNOLIA BAKERY PROMISES A MEMORABLE FUSION OF FLAVOR AND STYLE.
